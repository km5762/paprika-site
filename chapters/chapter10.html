Work and Wealth

 The DC Mini does not allow for automation within the movie and there are minimal implications of the device replacing occupations in psychotherapy related fields as it requires an operator. The product is unreleased and is not contributing to Globalization. In a way the Chairman’s abuse in the dream world creates a “winner takes all” society by idealizing himself as a perfect being and gaining power through trapping others in the dream world. It brings about the question of whether or not a “winner takes all” society can be brought without the relation to money in a virtual world. Due to this situation with the Chairman being the only notable example there is little to reference about Work and Wealth in Paprika.
