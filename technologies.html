Computing Technologies

  • Folding Camera: Used to capture light onto a photo-sensitive layer. Late 19th century.
  • Laptop: Portable computer. April 1981. Osborne 1
  • Automobiles: Allows for powered transportation. Benz Patent Motor Car was the first to be created. January 1886
  • Elevator: Safe passenger vertical lifting device. 1853
  • Handheld Cell Phone: Used to make calls wirelessly. Motorola DynaTAC 8000X was the first. 1983
  • Magnetic strip access card: Used to store information on a magnetic tape for identification purposes. 1960
  • Motorized wheelchair: Used for mobility impaired individuals. Klein Drive Chair was the first. 1953
  • Heart rate monitor/respiratory rate monitor: An ECG monitors electric stimulus through leads placed on the skin. 1887
  • Brain scan: Used to picture the brain. Different methods can reveal different parts of the complex structural make up. 1971
  • Interactive websites: The start of Web 2.0 marked the beginning of widespread user-facing websites and interactability. 1999
  • Oscilloscope: Allows for digital and analog frequencies to be displayed graphically. 1893
  • Wind up toys: Advances in battery technology allowed for electro-mechanical, kid-focused toys became available. 1960s
  • Brainwave imagery: Using brainwaves to reconstruct images that a person is thinking of is new emerging technology made possible by AI. 2023
  • Dc Mini: Fictional device that allows a user to enter a dream state. Can be networked together with other users.
